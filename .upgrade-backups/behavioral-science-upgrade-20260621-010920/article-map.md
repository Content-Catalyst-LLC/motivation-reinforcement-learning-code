# Motivation, Reinforcement, and Learning Article Map

Companion repository for motivation, reinforcement, learning, conditioning, feedback, reward systems, self-determination, gamification, and ethical learning environments.

## Articles

- [What Is Motivation?](articles/what-is-motivation/) — A foundational article on the forces that initiate and sustain behavior.
- [What Is Reinforcement?](articles/what-is-reinforcement/) — Reinforcement as behavioral strengthening through consequence and context.
- [Learning as Behavioral Adaptation](articles/learning-as-behavioral-adaptation/) — How organisms and people adjust behavior through experience.
- [Behaviorism and the Study of Observable Action](articles/behaviorism-and-observable-action/) — Classic behaviorism and empirical approaches to action.
- [From Conditioning to Cognitive Learning](articles/from-conditioning-to-cognitive-learning/) — The bridge between behaviorist learning and cognitive theory.
- [Motivation, Environment, and Agency](articles/motivation-environment-and-agency/) — How internal motives and external settings interact.
- [Classical Conditioning](articles/classical-conditioning/) — Learning through association and predictive cues.
- [Operant Conditioning](articles/operant-conditioning/) — Learning through consequences and behavioral selection.
- [Reinforcement and Punishment](articles/reinforcement-and-punishment/) — Strengthening and weakening behavior through consequences.
- [Positive and Negative Reinforcement](articles/positive-and-negative-reinforcement/) — How adding or removing stimuli can strengthen behavior.
- [Extinction, Generalization, and Discrimination](articles/extinction-generalization-and-discrimination/) — How learned behavior changes across contexts.
- [Schedules of Reinforcement](articles/schedules-of-reinforcement/) — Fixed, variable, interval, and ratio schedules.
- [Shaping, Chaining, and Behavioral Training](articles/shaping-chaining-and-behavioral-training/) — Building complex behaviors from simpler actions.
- [Reward Systems and Behavioral Strengthening](articles/reward-systems-and-behavioral-strengthening/) — How rewards affect behavior over time.
- [Feedback Loops in Learning](articles/feedback-loops-in-learning/) — Feedback as correction, reinforcement, and adaptation.
- [Immediate vs Delayed Feedback](articles/immediate-vs-delayed-feedback/) — Timing and the strength of learning signals.
- [Variable Rewards and Behavioral Capture](articles/variable-rewards-and-behavioral-capture/) — Unpredictable reward schedules, persistence, and platform risks.
- [Intrinsic and Extrinsic Motivation](articles/intrinsic-and-extrinsic-motivation/) — Internal and external drivers of action.
- [Self-Determination Theory](articles/self-determination-theory/) — Autonomy, competence, relatedness, and sustainable motivation.
- [Autonomy, Competence, and Relatedness](articles/autonomy-competence-and-relatedness/) — Core needs in self-determination theory.
- [Gamification and Behavioral Design](articles/gamification-and-behavioral-design/) — Points, feedback, incentives, motivation, and ethics.
